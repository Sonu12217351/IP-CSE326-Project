Donate Process
  1. Donor fills up the registration form and gives his consent for donation.
  2. Medical Check up (5 Mins) Donors Medical History & life style is asked, check up of temperature, blood pressure, pulse and haemoglobin.
  3. Donation (8 Mins) Phlebotomist draws unit (350ml/450ml) of blood.
  4. Refreshment (10 Mins)
